About

Marketing that holds its line.

I'm a digital marketing and content manager with 5+ years growing brands that live where the land meets the sea, from North Kiteboarding and North Foils to The Surfr App, Surf Life Saving New Zealand and the Bike Storage Company. I've captured content for Red Bull King of the Air, Red Bull Megaloop and the Cold Hawaii PWA World Cup, and I hold a BCom in Marketing and International Business from the University of Auckland.

My approach blends sharp performance marketing with content that genuinely belongs in the culture: multi-channel social, paid and organic strategy, copywriting and GA4-led reporting. The data tells me what to do more of, and knowing the sport tells me how to make it land.
